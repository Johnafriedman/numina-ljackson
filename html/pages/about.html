About Our Stamps

Leavenworth Jackson Rubber Stamps are hand-crafted in California of the finest rubber and premium maple wood blocks. Cushion is trimmed to rubber dies and stamp images are shown on top of the wood mounts. Much care is taken with preparation of artwork, engraving, rubber-smithing and trimming to make sure you obtain clearly detailed impressions from every stamp we offer. Our small workshop is committed to energy and resource conservation.
